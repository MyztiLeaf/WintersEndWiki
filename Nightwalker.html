Food

6 food pips are required to hibernate, while up to 9 may be stored for the next cycle.

Each Creature NightWalker drinks from them gives different pips count.

General Changes

  • NightWalker Has no overseer to track
  • NightWalker begins the game with neuron glow and the Mark of Communication.
  • NightWalker has Blood Bar based on biting creatures.

World Changes

  • NightWalker's campaign takes place in Pale Ascent region connection.

Abilities & Attributes

Revive Abilities

Nightwalker is able to revive other slugcats once, but if they die again they cannot be revived anymore.

Void Slam

By holding down and pressing jump, Nightwalker turns into void and performs a void slam, slamming into the ground and causing three spikes to erupt around it

Void Dash

By holding grab and either left or right and pressing jump, NightWalker performs a dash, dealing damage to [any creature/anything/everything] it passes through.

WallJump

NightWalker can WallJump indefinetly in any sides wall.

Nightwalker Walljump

WallHop

Nightwalker can WallHop after walljump will hop in mid air.

Nightwalker doing wallhop

Void Transformation

By performing [SPECIAL] If BloodBar is full, NightWalker can transform. Causing the rooms darkens. Able to stay longer away from sunlight.

Food

5 food pips are required to hibernate, while up to 5 may be stored for the next cycle.

The Snowflake inherits Hunter 's diet with some further changes:

  • Corpses give 1.25 their normal amount of food
  • Plants give 2 times their normal amount of food
  • Blue Fruit Blue Fruit and Snow Berry and its derivatives give 3.5 food pips
  • Centipedes give 2.25 times their normal amount of food

General Changes

  • Snowflake is followed by a white/gray overseer
  • Snowflake can create Ice Spears at the cost of one food pip
  • Snowflake holds items in the middle of their body like a Slugpup Slugpup. This is intentional.

World Changes

Snowflake's campaign takes place entirely within regions disconnected from the base game.

  • This means that normal regions and iterators, such as Five Pebbles (region) Five Pebbles and Looks to the Moon (region) Looks to the Moon are completely inaccessible by Snowflake without cheats.

The world is cold enough that Blizzard rampage, though Snowflake does not lose warmth as fast as Saint Saint and can warm themselves up on their own.

  • The rain timer cannot be seen as a result.

Abilities & Attributes

Back Spear

Hunter , Snowflake can store spears on their back by holding grab.

Double Jump

By holding grab and jump while in the air, Snowflake can perform a prolongued double jump that varies in height depending on the player's hypothermia. This double jump has a short cooldown afterwards.

Snowflake's double jump ability

Slide Pounce Attack

By slide pouncing, Snowflake can attack Lizards Lizards (specifically lizards, though) on long slide.

Snowflake's Belly slides

SnowPuffs

By holding down and grab, after pressing jump, Snowflake can release Snowpuffs, it puffs out into snow clouds, that obscure Creature vision. Will fade away within 10 seconds.

Snowflake's releasing SnowPuffs clouds

Warmth

Snowflake can heal themselves of hypothermia by holding down for a while. This causes them to curl up and begin sleeping and slowly brings back their warmth.

Stats

  • Snowflake uses Survivor Survivor's stats, except that pole climbing is slightly faster (1 instead of 0.85).
  • Additionally, Snowflake has a speed boost when crawling on all fours and not in a corridor.

Story

SPOILER WARNING
This section contains major story spoilers for Snowflake. Proceed with caution!

Campaign

Snowflake begins their journey in Blizzard's Embrace and eventually makes their way to the iterator Cryobloom Mist Cryobloom Mist, who is suffering power malfunctions from the cold. Cryobloom Mist gives Snowflake the Mark and max karma as well as a custom drone and tells them to restore power to the facility.

Ending

Upon restoring power to Cryobloom Mist, Snowflake makes their way back to Cryobloom Mist. If the player holds down while in the chamber, the ending plays.

Tips

After using Double jump, Using a Ice Spear Resets the Double jump and can use in mid air.
